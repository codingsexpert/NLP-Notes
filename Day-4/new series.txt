Why NLP Matters
NLP is described as a critical skill for creating intelligent systems that can process human language. Real-world applications discussed include:
•	Spam Classification: Distinguishing between junk and legitimate emails.
•	Search Engine Optimization: How Google uses knowledge graphs to provide relevant search results and recommendations.
•	Language Translation: The mechanics behind tools like Google Translator.
•	Chatbots: Developing conversational agents that can understand context and intent.
